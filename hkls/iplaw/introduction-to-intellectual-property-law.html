Introduction to Intellectual Property Law

Enthusiastic and ambitious business entrepreneurs start their businesses with great products, technology and business ideas. These key assets of their company are however, not given a sufficient degree of protection because of their apparent lack of legal knowledge. For instance, they might have shared the whole business method, product in pitching presentations to potential investors and partners without acquiring adequate legal protection on their rights. All these potential rooms of infringements of their work, as well as possible legal actions in future will significantly affect the value of their businesses. On the other hand, use of social media has become inevitable nowadays, intellectual property issues and disputes may arise when business entrepreneurs create contents to promote their business in these websites. Therefore, the articles aim to provide basic knowledge of intellectual property law, so that business entrepreneurs can gain early-stage protection of their products and ideas.

According to the World Intellectual Property Organization (WIPO), intellectual property (IP) protects creations of the mind, such as inventions; literary and artistic works; designs; and symbols, names and images used in commerce. IP is protected in various ways and below is an overview of the different forms of IP.

Type of IP What is it? What does it generally protect Is registration required?
Patent
  • An innovative technological invention, such as a drug, a new technology
 Yes
Copyright
  • Literary, dramatic, musical or artistic work
  • But it protects the expression of idea, not the idea itself
No
Registered Design
  • Appearance of a product, including the packaging and arrangement
  • But it protects the article's ornamental purposes only
 Yes
Trade Mark
  • Everything that represents the business and its goodwill, such as the company name, logo, the package of the product
 Yes
Trade Secret
  • Confidential business information such as formulae, business method, business plans and clients lists
No
